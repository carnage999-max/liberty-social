The **Risk Score** is a **0-100 percentage** that tells buyers how likely a listing is a **scam or fraudulent**. It's a trust indicator.

---

## HOW IT'S CALCULATED

Think of it like a **red flag counter**. Each red flag adds points:

| Red Flag | Points Added | Why? |
|----------|--------------|------|
| **No photos** | +15 | Scammers often don't provide real photos |
| **Each scam report** | +10 per flag | Other users marked it as suspicious |
| **Seller not verified** | +20 | Unverified sellers are higher risk |
| **No vet documents** (paid animals) | +10 | Legitimate sellers provide health docs |
| **Brand new seller** (< 7 days) | +10 | New accounts are riskier |
| **Too many "sold" relists** (>10) | +15 | Pattern of repeated selling suggests fraud |

**Maximum score: 100** (very risky)
